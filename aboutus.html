About HalalNYC

HalalNYC is a simple and user-friendly website built to help Muslims in New York City easily find halal food options and connect with local halal restaurants.

Our Mission

Whether you're a student, commuter, or local resident, HalalNYC is here to guide you to delicious, certified halal meals throughout the five boroughs. We aim to bridge the gap between busy lifestyles and halal accessibility by creating a smooth, informative browsing experience for all.

Our Love for Food

We’re more than just developers—we’re food lovers with a deep appreciation for rich flavors and diverse culinary traditions. Growing up in New York, we’ve shared late-night shawarmas, spicy chicken over rice, and warm conversations over sizzling platters. HalalNYC is a reflection of that love—our tribute to the halal food culture that brings people together.
